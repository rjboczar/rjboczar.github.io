Bio

I am a fourth-year PhD student in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at UC Berkeley, advised by Prof. Ben Recht, working in BAIR, and supported by the DoD NDSEG Fellowship. I am interested in optimization and other areas of applied mathematics. This past summer, I hung out at Google.

Last years, I was a GSI for the first major-agnostic introductory data science course. During the course, I spent some time cramming machine learning classifiers into Tweets.

Previously, I worked in the RISE Lab at NAVAIR in Lakehurst, NJ. Prior to that, I received my M.S. in Electrical Engineering along with a B.S. in Electrical Engineering and a minor in Mathematics from the University of Pennsylvania.

I enjoy basketball and music.
